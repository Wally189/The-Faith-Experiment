A public experiment in Catholic self-education

Foundations of the Catholic faith

A guided journey through Joseph Deharbe’s substantial catechism, read carefully alongside Scripture, the modern Catechism and current Church teaching.

This course is built around Joseph Deharbe’s A Complete Catechism of the Catholic Religion. Although it is an older work, it offers a clear and substantial introduction to the Catholic faith. Deharbe provides the route through the subject; Scripture, the modern Catechism and current Church teaching guide how it is understood today.

Schedule

The aim is to read slowly enough to remember. A normal lesson should be spread across reading, handwritten recall, source checking and a short explanation in your own words.

A sensible rhythm

Three sessions each week

Session 1: encounter and Deharbe reading

Session 2: notebook, distinctions and objections

Session 3: review and demonstration

Course blocks

1. God and Revelation

Reason, desire, revelation, Scripture and Tradition.

2. Christ and Salvation

Incarnation, Cross, Resurrection, grace and redemption.

3. Sacraments and Christian Life

Worship, sacramental life, virtue and conscience.

4. Prayer and Mission

Prayer, holiness, social doctrine and lay vocation.
